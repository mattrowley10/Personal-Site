About Me

Herbalist

For the past 10 years, the continued study and practice of herbal medicine has driven my passion and propelled my desire to be a diverse healer for my family, friends, and people of my community.

Musician

The sounds of music have permeated my being from the moment I was born. From banging on pots and pans to picking up my first acoustic guitar and playing a real drum set, my love for music continues to grow. I play drums and guitar, produce curated beats and sounds through Ableton, sing, and write music.

Creator

Whether I am crafting a song of love, brewing a magic potion, or learning how to commune with computers, I am in a perpetual state of learning how to create in all of the ways that inspire me.
